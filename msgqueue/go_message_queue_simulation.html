Operation Glossary

Slots (`chan struct{}`) Backpressure mechanism. A token/slot must be acquired to "Publish". If all slots are full, publishers wait at the gate.
Mutex (`sync.Mutex`) Critical section lock. Only one publisher or worker can modify the `msgs` slice at any given time to avoid data races.
Cond (`sync.Cond.Wait`) Notification variable. Workers sleep (`Wait`) when the queue is empty. When a message is published, a (`Broadcast`) wakes them up.
*Tip: Click "Publish Msg" repeatedly to fill the buffer and watch the backpressure mechanism block incoming messages!
